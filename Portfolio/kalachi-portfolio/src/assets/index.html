I am currently a senior lecturer in the Department of Computer Engineering, at the National Advanced school of Engineering - Yaounde, University of Yaounde 1. Before I was a post-doctoral researcher at Inria in the ARIC team at ENS de Lyon, involved in the RISQ project and working with Prof. Damien Stehlé on lattice-based cryptography. I did my PhD in the teams C & A (Université de Rouen-Normandie) and ERAL (University of Yaounde 1), under the supervision of Prof. Ayoub Otmani and Prof. Selestin Ndjeya.
